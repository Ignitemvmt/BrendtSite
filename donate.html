Partner With Our Ministry

Your generosity helps us build up and prepare the way of the Lord

"Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap." — Luke 6:38

Your generous donations help us continue our intercession ministry across the 8 counties of Kansas, the USA, and globally. Every gift, no matter the size, makes a difference in building up and preparing the way for God's people—helping them become free and powerful in Christ.

Our Vision

Where Your Giving Goes

Local Intercession Ministry

Supporting prayer and intercession work across 8 counties in Kansas, bringing spiritual awakening and transformation to our communities.

Traveling Ministry

Enabling Pastor Brendt and Kim to travel across the USA and internationally to speak, teach, and minister at churches and conferences.

International Missions

Supporting ministry trips abroad, including ongoing work in Belize and future international intercession and teaching assignments.

Future Rehab Facility

Building toward our vision of establishing a faith-based rehabilitation facility in Coffeyville, Kansas to help those struggling with addiction find freedom in Christ.
