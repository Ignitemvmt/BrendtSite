International Ministry

Teaching & Intercession Abroad

Pastor Brendt has traveled abroad to Belize as a pastor, assisting with international intercession assignments and bringing Biblical teaching to communities hungry for the Word.

Through these international missions, Pastor Brendt provides training, mentorship, and spiritual guidance to local churches and ministries, helping to equip leaders and believers for the work of the Kingdom.

These assignments often focus on prayer, intercession, and teaching foundational Biblical truths that help believers grow in their faith and become free and powerful in Christ.

Traveling Ministry Across the USA

Brendt and Kim travel throughout the United States to speak at churches, conferences, and ministry events. Their heart is to see believers set free and walking in the power of God.

As they continue to grow in their calling, they are preparing for an even greater evangelistic ministry—one that will take them to more churches, more cities, and more nations with the message of freedom and power in Christ.

Their vision is to expand their traveling ministry, reaching more people with the Gospel and equipping believers to walk in the fullness of what God has for them.

Our Vision for the Future

We believe God is calling us to expand our reach and impact in significant ways. Beyond our current ministry in Kansas and across the USA, we have a vision for something even greater.

Future Vision: Rehab Facility in Coffeyville

We have a dream to establish a rehabilitation facility in Coffeyville, Kansas—a place where people struggling with addiction and life-controlling issues can find freedom, healing, and restoration through the power of Jesus Christ. This facility would provide a safe, faith-based environment for transformation.

As we continue to build up and prepare the way of the Lord, we trust God to open doors and provide the resources needed to see this vision become reality.
